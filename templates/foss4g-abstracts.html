What is this site?

The FOSS4G community review is a shared effort to evaluate all the 400 abstracts submitted in a fair process. The system randomizes the list to give everyone the same chance to get votes so your list will be different from everyone else.

This site gives you in a single page the full list of abstracts along with the page on the evaluation system and the current score you've submitted. This will be helfpul for those willing to provide feedback to selected talks avoiding to traverse the 20 pages of abstracts.
